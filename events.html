Spring Clean Bachman Lake Join Keep Dallas Beautiful on My Park day as we beautify Bachman Lake. Both staff and general volunteers are needed for the event. Staff will manage volunteer check-in and lead teams through litter clean-up, planting, weeding and spreading mulch.

Annual Tri-City Cleanup of Lake Hubbard. This lake is the main water supply for the city of Dallas, yet it is bounded by other cities. Keep Rowlett Beautiful, Keep Garland Beautiful and Keep Dallas Beautiful have partnered to improve the quality of life for people and wildlife who all depend on clean water in Lake Hubbard for survival.
